About Us

The Boardgamery is a coffee shop and cereal bar featuring over 300 playable board games. Our menu includes a variety of delicious coffee, tea, frozen drinks and mouth-watering treats such as cookies, candies and over 20 flavors of cereal! Purchase a play pass for 3$ to gain access for the day to our huge board game library and play as many games as you want! Children 12 and under play for free (when accompanied by a paying adult). We are connected to the Novel Idea Bookstore, so while you are here, feel free to take your food, beverage or board game down the hall and browse hundreds of books, records, movies and more!
